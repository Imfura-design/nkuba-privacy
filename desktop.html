Privacy Policy — Nkuba

Desktop application · Last updated: May 2026
See also: Nkuba Helper browser extension policy

This policy explains what the Nkuba desktop application does with information on your device. In short: the app collects no personal data, has no accounts, sends nothing to the developer, and has no analytics, advertising or tracking.

What the app does

Nkuba downloads videos from web links you provide — by pasting a link, or by receiving one from the companion Nkuba Helper browser extension — and saves them to your computer for personal offline viewing. Everything the app does runs locally on your own device.

Information the app handles

The app processes, and stores only on your device:

  • The video links you choose — to fetch and download the video you asked for.
  • A local download list and history — the title, source link and saved file location of your downloads, kept so the app can show your downloads and let you re-open files. This is stored in the app's local data folder on your computer.
  • Your settings — such as the download folder, quality and format preferences — stored locally.

None of this is sent to the developer or to any third party. You can clear the download history at any time from within the app.

Network access

The app connects to the internet only to:

  • Download the video you requested — by contacting the website or media server that hosts that video.
  • Update its download engine — the app uses the open-source yt-dlp engine and can download updates to it from that project's official distribution source.

The app also runs a small server on the loopback address (127.0.0.1) of your own computer so the companion browser extension can hand it a chosen video. This connection never leaves your machine. The app performs no background reporting and contacts no analytics or advertising service.

Protected content

Nkuba detects video that is protected by digital rights management (DRM) and refuses to download it. It does not decrypt, circumvent or weaken DRM or any other technical protection measure.

Data retention

No data is retained by the developer because none is collected. Your settings and download history remain on your device until you clear them or uninstall the app.

Children

Nkuba is intended for adults (18 or older, or the age of majority where you live). It is not directed at children.

Changes

This policy may be updated for future versions of the app. The current version is published alongside the app's store listing.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the app's store page.
